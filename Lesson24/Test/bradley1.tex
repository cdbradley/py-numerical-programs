\title{Gregory Series}
\author{}
\date{}
\maketitle

The Gregory series for $\pi$ is

\begin{equation}
\pi = 4 \sum_{k=1}^{\infty} \frac{(-1)^{k+1}}{2k-1} \label{Gregory_Series}
\end{equation}

The series (\ref{Gregory_Series}) is obtained by plugging $x=1$ into the Leibniz series for $\tan^{-1}x$.
